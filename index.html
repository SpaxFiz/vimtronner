Greetings, Programs! vimtronner is a multiplayer, real-time, command-line game that teaches you the core vim keys.

Be the last player alive by either controlling your bike safely around obstacles or enter INSERT mode to build your own walls for your opponents to crash into. Just remember, you can't do both at the same time!

Learn how to install and play!

Inspired by Patrick Moody's vimsnake and, of course, TRON. Read about how we built vimtronner in Node.js and Socket.IO.
